Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about SoulConnect

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our algorithm considers your interests, values, personality traits, and preferences to find compatible matches. The more you use the platform and interact with profiles, the better our suggestions become.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take privacy and security seriously. All profiles are verified, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Can I use SoulConnect for free?

Yes, we offer a free tier that allows you to create a profile, browse matches, and send limited likes. For unlimited messaging and advanced features, we offer Premium and VIP subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time from the settings page. We'll be sad to see you go, but the process is simple and immediate.
