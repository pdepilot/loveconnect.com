Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and subscriptions

Why are there different pricing tiers for men and women?

Our pricing structure is designed to create a balanced user experience. Female users typically receive more attention on dating platforms, so our pricing reflects this dynamic while ensuring everyone has access to features that match their dating goals.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll immediately gain access to the new features. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay for your convenience. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

Is there a free trial for VIP plans?

We occasionally offer free trials for our VIP plans during promotional periods. Check our website or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on current offers.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your VIP features until the end of your current billing period.
