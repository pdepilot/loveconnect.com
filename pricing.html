Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our subscription plans

Why are there different pricing tiers for males and females?

Our pricing structure is designed to create a balanced user experience. Typically, dating platforms have more male users, so we've structured our pricing to encourage more female participation while providing value for all users regardless of gender.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Is there a free trial for VIP plans?

We occasionally offer free trials for our VIP plans during promotional periods. Check our website or subscribe to our newsletter to stay informed about current promotions.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period.
